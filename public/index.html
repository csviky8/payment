Educational payment plans

PulsePass lets learners choose a plan and pay with Razorpay instantly.

Choose a plan, sign up, and pay through Razorpay Checkout. The MVP keeps the UX clear while leaving payment logic secure on the server.

What RP means

RP stands for Renewal Pass. It is an internal entitlement unit used to map plan length to access value.

  • Monthly: 1 month, 1 RP
  • 3-Month: 3 months, 2 RP
  • Yearly: 1 year, 4 RP

User flow

How the payment journey works

  1. Visitor lands on the site and reviews plans.
  2. User can optionally save name and email, but payment works without it.
  3. Frontend asks the backend to create a Razorpay order.
  4. Razorpay Checkout collects payment details and returns the payment signature.
  5. Backend verifies the signature with the order id and payment id.
  6. The success page shows the verified order and payment reference.
